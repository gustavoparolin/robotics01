### Accordion: When Do I Need to Renew?
Your Robotics Technician — Class A, B, or C certificate is valid for three years from the date it was issued or last renewed. To renew your certificate, log in to your Online Services account. If you do not have an account, you will need to register for one.

### Accordion: What Happens if I Do Not Renew?
If your certificate expires, you may be subject to the following:
- A safety officer may issue a compliance order or monetary penalty
- A safety officer may order you to stop work immediately
- You will be required to apply for a new certificate, which includes applicable fees
- You may be required to successfully complete the certification exam and interview

### Accordion: How Do I Update My Skills Passport?
Updating your Skills Passport is encouraged as it provides evidence of your competence and experience. While not mandatory for renewal, we recommend you submit your Skills Passport paperwork at the time of renewal.

Class-specific declaration forms are available for:
- Class A — Industrial Robots
- Class B — Service and Autonomous Robots
- Class C — Drone Systems

Declaration forms must be signed by a designated signing officer.

### Accordion: Continuing Education Requirements
You must complete a minimum of 24 hours of continuing education during your three-year certificate period. Training must be approved by Technical Safety BC.

The 24 hours are divided as follows:
- 6 hours of trade-specific safety training
- 6 hours of training on the Act, regulations, and code
- 12 hours of technical training specific to your certificate class
