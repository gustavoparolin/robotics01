Advancing Robotics Safety

We oversee the safety of industrial robots, service robots, delivery drones, autonomous vehicles, surgical robots, and warehouse automation systems in accordance with the Safety Standards Act and the Robotics Safety Regulation.

Robotics Regulations

The regulatory framework for robotics in British Columbia includes:

  • Safety Standards Act
  • Safety Standards General Regulation
  • Robotics Safety Regulation
  • Fee Setting Criteria Regulation
  • Monetary Penalties Regulation

CSA R100 Robotics Safety Code

As of April 30, 2025, Technical Safety BC has adopted the CSA R100-25 Robotics Safety Code. All licensed contractors are required to have the current code book.

Reference codes: CSA R100, ISO 10218, ISO 13482, CAN/CSA-Z434
